Quotes are estimates based on the information you provide and are subject to aircraft and crew availability. Quotes are typically valid for a limited period, as specified. To secure a flight, you must sign a charter agreement with the designated carrier and make payment per its terms. Payment methods and timelines will be outlined in your invoice. Bookings are confirmed only once the signed agreement and payment are received by the carrier.

Cancellation policies are determined by the operating carrier and will be detailed in your charter agreement. Fees may apply depending on when you cancel. Refunds are processed according to the carrier's policy, with VELARION Skies assisting as needed. Itinerary changes are subject to aircraft availability and may incur additional costs.
